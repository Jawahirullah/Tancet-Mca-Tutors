                                                                                                      Divisibility Rule

                                                                                                      This is a technique or a method, which you can use to find all the divisors of a particular number. By this rule, you can easily find, by which number you can divide a particular number.

                                                                                                      Here we have some rules to find the divisors up to 19.

                                                                                                      Rules of Divisibility Rule

                                                                                                      5, 10

                                                                                                      1. If a number ends with 0 or 5, then it is divided by 5.
                                                                                                      2. If a number ends with 0 then it is divided by 10.

                                                                                                      Examples

                                                                                                    • 2345 east Ends with 5, hence the number 2345 divided by 5.
                                                                                                    • 2340 east Ends with 5, hence the number 2345 divided by 0.

                                                                                                      • 2

                                                                                                      1. If any number ends with even digit, then that number is divided by 2.

                                                                                                      Examples

                                                                                                      • 52 east Ends with 2(even number), hence the number 52 divided by 2.

                                                                                                      3, 9

                                                                                                      1. If Sum of all digits of a number divided by 3, then whole number is divided by 3.
                                                                                                      2. If Sum of all digits of a number divided by 9, then whole number is divided by 9.

                                                                                                      Examples

                                                                                                      • 45 east 4 + 5 = 9. 9 divided by 3 equal to 0. Hence, 45 is divided by 3.
                                                                                                      • 117 east 1 + 1 + 7 = 9. 9 divided by 3 equal to 0. Hence, 117 is divided by 3.

                                                                                                      4, 8

                                                                                                      1. If last two digits of a number divided by 4, then whole number is divided by 4.
                                                                                                      2. If last three digits of a number divided by 8, then whole number is divided by 8.

                                                                                                      Examples

                                                                                                      • 1332 east 32 / 2 = 0. Hence, 1332 is divided by 4.
                                                                                                      • 1024 east 024 / 8 = 0. Hence, 1024 is divided by 8.

                                                                                                      7, 13, 17, 19

                                                                                                      These numbers are little bit tricky to find divisor of number.

                                                                                                      1. Take the last digit of number and multiply that number with 2 or 4 or 5 depend on which divisible you want to find.
                                                                                                        • For example, if you want to find, If a number can be divided by 7 or 19, you have to multiply last digit with 2.
                                                                                                        • If you want to find, if a number can be divided by 13, multiply last digit with 4.
                                                                                                        • If you want to find a number can be divided by 17, multiply last digit with 5.
                                                                                                      2. Add or Minus the rest of digits from the result obtained by above process.

                                                                                                      Example for 7 :

                                                                                                      343

                                                                                                      Here last digit of 343 is 3. Rest of digits are 34.

                                                                                                      34 - (3 x 2) = 28

                                                                                                      28 / 7 = 0.

                                                                                                      Hence, the number 343 is divisible by 7.

                                                                                                      Example for 13 :

                                                                                                      325

                                                                                                      Here last digit of 325 is 5. Rest of digits are 32.

                                                                                                      32 + (5 x 4) = 52

                                                                                                      52 / 13 = 0.

                                                                                                      Hence, the number 325 is divisible by 13.

                                                                                                      Example for 17 :

                                                                                                      1904

                                                                                                      Here last digit of 1904 is 4. Rest of digits are 190.

                                                                                                      190 - (4 x 5) = 170

                                                                                                      170 / 17 = 0.

                                                                                                      Hence, the number 1904 is divisible by 17.

                                                                                                      Example for 19 :

                                                                                                      361

                                                                                                      Here last digit of 361 is 1. Rest of digits are 36.

                                                                                                      36 + (1 x 2) = 38

                                                                                                      38 / 19 = 0.

                                                                                                      Hence, the number 361 is divisible by 19.

                                                                                                      Respective Multiplication Numbers for 7, 13, 17, 19.
                                                                                                      • 7 east Multiply with 2 east Subtract
                                                                                                      • 13 east Multiply with 4 east Add
                                                                                                      • 17 east Multiply with 5 east Subtact
                                                                                                      • 19 east Multiply with 2 east Add

                                                                                                      16

                                                                                                      For sixteen, multiply first digit of number by 4. And add rest of digits to the result of multiply.

                                                                                                      Example

                                                                                                      176 east (1 x 4) + 76 = 80

                                                                                                      80 / 16 = 0

                                                                                                      In this example, 1 is the first digit of 176. You have to multiply the 1 (i,e first digit) with result of digits, that is 76.

                                                                                                      the result 80 is divided by 16, the whole number 176 is divided by 16.

                                                                                                      11

                                                                                                      For 11, you have to use one formula. That is,
                                                                                                      ( Sum of all odd digits ) - (sum of all even digits).

                                                                                                      Example

                                                                                                      286 east (2 + 6) - 8

                                                                                                      8 - 8 = 0

                                                                                                      Hence, the number 286 is divided by 11.

                                                                                                      6, 12, 14, 15, 18

                                                                                                      These numbers don't have any unique methods to find divisibles. A given number is divided by these numbers, if that given number divided by smallest and largest factor of these numbers.

                                                                                                      Below are the pairs of numbers for every number.

                                                                                                      • 6 - (2, 3)
                                                                                                      • 12 - (4, 3)
                                                                                                      • 14 - (7, 2)
                                                                                                      • 15 - (5, 3)
                                                                                                      • 18 - (9, 2)

                                                                                                      Example

                                                                                                      36

                                                                                                      To check whether the number 36 is divided by 6 or not, we have to multiply it by both 2 and 3.
                                                                                                      As 36 is divisible by both 2 and 3, it is also divisible by 6.

                                                                                                      The above number is similar to all the other numbers, such as 6, 12, 14, 15, 18

                                                                                                      These are the rules to find out whether the given number can be divided by particular number or not.
